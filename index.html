Web Development Portfolio


Job Search

Product of group project #1 app that allows searching for government jobs while viewing quality of life data

The Homies app allows job seekers to search for federal job opportunities in major US cities while receiving detailed quality of life data on those locations. Quality of life data includes 16 categories ranging from housing to education to tolerance level to art and culture and scored on a 10-point scale. Local storage was implemented to store the user's last visit date and last search criteria

Job search is powered by API from USAJobs.org while quality of life data was provided by Teleport.org API.

Weather Dashboard

Get a 5-day weather forecast in any city around the world

The Weather Dashboard app leverages API from OpenWeather.org to search weather in all major cities around the globe and retreive a detailed 5-day weather. Local storage saves the last ten cities searched.

Interestingly, a second API had to be used to fetch UV Index data and then conditional statments were utilizied to color the UV value to indicate severity level.
